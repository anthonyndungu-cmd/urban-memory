Know your Music

Who We Are

Music Time is a fan-run hub built for people who don't just listen to music — they follow it. We track the artists, the releases, and the moments that make the scene worth paying attention to, across genres and borders. We're not a label. We're not affiliated with any artist, agency, or record company. We're fans who wanted a single place to keep up with the music we care about — and figured other people probably wanted the same thing.

Why We Started This

Music moves fast. A single drops, a feature surprises everyone, an artist plays a show three cities away — and by the time you hear about it, it's old news. Music Time exists to close that gap: one place for spotlights, updates, and show dates, without having to dig through a dozen different pages to find them.

What You'll Find Here

  • profiles — backgrounds, breakthroughs, and what makes each one worth knowing
  • News — releases, collaborations, and announcements as they happen
  • Performances — where to catch your favorites live
  • A growing list of genres — because good music doesn't stay in one lane

Our Approach

Everything here is written by us, based on public information, official releases, and our own listening. We aim to be accurate and fair — and where we get something wrong, we'll correct it. If you're an artist, label, or rights holder with a concern about anything on this site, [reach out →] and we'll address it directly.

Who's Behind It

Music Time is built and maintained by a small team (or: by one person, adjust as needed) passionate about discovering and sharing great music across scenes that don't always get equal coverage. This site started as a school web development project and grew into something we genuinely enjoy maintaining.
